About

Empirical economics of firms and productivity

Mariana Pereira-López is a Senior Economist in the Private Markets Group within the Development Economics Vice Presidency (DEC) at the World Bank Group. Her research focuses on firm-level dynamics and productivity, management practices, technology adoption, innovation, entrepreneurship, impact evaluations, competition, energy efficiency, startup ecosystems, and venture capital dynamics.

Her work has been published in leading journals, including the American Economic Journal: Macroeconomics, PNAS, the Journal of Development Economics, Research Policy, the World Bank Economic Review, and the Journal of Environmental Economics and Management.

She holds a Ph.D. and an M.A. in Economics from El Colegio de México and has more than sixteen years of experience across government, academia, and international organizations.
